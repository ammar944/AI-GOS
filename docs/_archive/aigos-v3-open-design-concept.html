AI-GOS should own supervised GTM research, not generic AI automation.

The sharper product promise is that AI-GOS turns a company link into a supervised GTM research run: it preserves onboarding context, opens sources, tracks evidence quality, and writes artifacts the operator can approve or revise. 1

Positioning thesis

Most alternatives collapse research, reasoning, and writing into an opaque answer. AI-GOS should make the research process visible enough to trust, while keeping the surface quiet enough to feel like a coworker writing a report.

Important architecture: worker persists corpus/context/sources/gaps. The Vercel AI SDK workspace agent synthesizes the visible report. Old six-card v2 schema is not the primary surface.

Open gap

Before claiming lift, the product needs concrete before/after proof. Unsupported claims stay in gaps, not the final synthesis. 2
