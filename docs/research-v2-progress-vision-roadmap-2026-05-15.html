North star

AI-GOS should feel like an AI-native GTM operator, not a dashboard.

The customer is not buying a pile of research. They are buying the confidence to pitch, position, and activate a company with evidence.

Product thesis

Company context in, audit out.

The user gives a URL and confirms the business context. AI-GOS researches the market, buyer, competitors, voice of customer, demand, and offer health, then produces one coherent audit.

Interaction thesis

Read the artifact, command the agent.

The primary surface is a centered artifact reader. Chat exists as an artifact command line for tightening claims, rerunning sections, exposing sources, and later generating activation assets.

Business thesis

Sell the audit before expanding the suite.

The first paid wedge should be the Pre-Pitch Positioning Audit. Activation layers such as media plans, scripts, landing pages, and sales assets should attach after the audit is trusted.

Current setup

The system is async, persisted, and audit-state driven.

The UI should stay thin: it reads audit state, renders the artifact, and sends focused commands. The worker owns the research and typed output work.

Risks and decisions

Do not lose the shape we just earned.

The main risk is not that the backend cannot produce sections anymore. The risk is that the product reads like an internal tool instead of a finished audit.

Known build blocker

npm run build compiles and passes TypeScript, then fails during /_not-found prerender because NEXT_PUBLIC_CLERK_PUBLISHABLE_KEY is missing in this local environment.

Locked product direction

Keep the primary surface as a centered artifact reader. Avoid returning to a debug dashboard, three-pane workspace, or telemetry-first layout unless the user explicitly asks for an operator console.

Risk

Typed output exists, but trust depends on UI evidence.

Users need to see sources, confidence, gaps, and progress in the artifact itself. Hidden backend success is not product success.

Risk

Synthesis is still missing.

Six strong Sections are not yet a final recommendation. The next value jump is the cross-section verdict that says what to do.
