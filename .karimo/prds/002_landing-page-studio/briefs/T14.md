### Deliverable 2: google-fonts.json

Location: `skills/landing-page-studio/data/google-fonts.json`

Structure:

```json
{
  "version": "1.0.0",
  "description": "Curated Google Fonts pairs for SaaS landing pages. Used by direction picker (T5) to constrain font choices.",
  "pairs": [
    {
      "id": "inter-inter",
      "heading": "Inter",
      "body": "Inter",
      "style": "modern-minimal",
      "tags": ["tech", "saas", "clean"]
    },
    ...39 more pairs...
  ]
}
```

**Curation criteria for the 40 pairs:**
- All fonts must be available on Google Fonts (https://fonts.google.com)
- Heading + body must be harmonious (same family is fine, e.g. Inter/Inter)
- Cover a range of styles: modern-minimal, editorial, humanist, geometric, serif-forward
- Bias toward fonts that render well at both large heading sizes and small body sizes
- No decorative or display-only fonts (nothing that breaks at body size)
- Include at least:
  - 10 pairs using Inter, Outfit, or Plus Jakarta Sans as heading
  - 5 pairs with a serif heading (e.g. Playfair Display, Lora, DM Serif Display)
  - 5 pairs suitable for finance/enterprise tone
  - 5 pairs suitable for consumer/startup tone

Each pair must have: `id` (kebab-case, unique), `heading`, `body`, `style` (one of: modern-minimal, editorial, humanist, geometric, serif-forward), `tags` (array of strings).

**The 40 pairs must be written by the implementer** based on knowledge of Google Fonts. Do not hallucinate font names — verify each font exists at fonts.google.com before including it. If uncertain about a font's existence, use Inter as a fallback.
