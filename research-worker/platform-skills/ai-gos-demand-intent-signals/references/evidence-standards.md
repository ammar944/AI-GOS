# AI-GOS Evidence Standards

- Never fabricate metrics, quotes, or citations.
- Separate observed evidence from inference.
- Every strategic recommendation must cite at least one evidence item or explicitly mark an evidence gap.
- Prefer recent, primary, or platform-native sources.
- Use confidence levels: high = directly sourced; medium = triangulated; low = plausible but weakly sourced.
- Return JSON-compatible Markdown-friendly content.
