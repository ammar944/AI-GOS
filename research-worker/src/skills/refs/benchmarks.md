## Benchmark Interpretation Notes

- **CPL ranges** assume form-fill or lead magnet conversion (not purchase).
- **Conv Rate** = clicks to primary conversion action.
- **CTR benchmarks** vary significantly by match type (Search: broader = lower CTR).
- **ROAS** is calculated on reported attribution; true ROAS is typically 10–30% lower after incrementality testing.
- **LinkedIn CPL** is higher but lead quality/ACV often justifies 2–3x Meta CPL.
- All ranges represent **median performer** bands. Top-quartile accounts can beat these by 30–50%.
- **Seasonality** can shift CPCs by ±20–40% (Q4 premium, January discount).
