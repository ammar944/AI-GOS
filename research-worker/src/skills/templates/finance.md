# Finance / Fintech Media Plan Template

> Source: Industry benchmark aggregates (2024–2025). Applies to financial services including
> banking, lending, insurance, investment platforms, accounting software, and fintech apps.
> COMPLIANCE NOTE: Financial advertising is heavily regulated. Legal review is mandatory.

---

## Compliance Requirements (Read First)

### Required Disclosures by Product Type

| Product Type          | Required Disclosures                                                   |
|-----------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Lending / Credit      | APR range, loan terms, "rates vary by credit score", state licensing  |
| Insurance             | Coverage details, exclusions summary, licensing numbers (by state)     |
| Investment products   | "Capital at risk", "Past performance not indicative of future results" |
| Credit cards          | APR, annual fee, intro period terms                                    |
| Mortgage              | APR, term, qualifying requirements                                     |
| Tax services          | "Individual results may vary" for refund-related claims               |
| Crypto / Digital assets| "Not FDIC insured", "Highly speculative" risk warnings               |

### Platform Policy Notes

- **Meta Special Ad Category — Credit**: Required for all credit products (credit cards, loans, mortgage). Eliminates age, gender, and ZIP-code exclusions; limits lookalike targeting.
- **Google Certification**: Financial product advertisers must comply with Google's Financial products and services policy; some lending products require country-specific certification.
- **LinkedIn**: Fewer restrictions than Meta/Google but claims must be accurate and substantiated.
- **Crypto / NFT**: Most platforms heavily restrict; some require pre-authorization.

## Campaign Architecture

### Google Ads Structure

```
Account
├── Campaign: Brand Search
│   └── Ad Group: [Brand] + product type exact
│
├── Campaign: Competitor Search
│   ├── Ad Group: [Competitor A] + alternative/compare
│   ├── Ad Group: [Competitor B] + switch/better rates
│   └── Ad Group: [Legacy competitor] modern alternative
│
├── Campaign: Product/Intent Search
│   ├── Ad Group: [Product type] apply / rates / compare
│   ├── Ad Group: [Product] for [use case/audience]
│   └── Ad Group: [Problem] solution keywords
│
├── Campaign: Calculator / Tool Intent
│   ├── Ad Group: [Product] calculator / estimator
│   └── Ad Group: "how much" + [financial question]
│
└── Campaign: Remarketing
    ├── Ad Group: Application starts (7-day)
    ├── Ad Group: Calculator users (14-day)
    └── Ad Group: Pricing page visitors (30-day)
```

### Meta Ads Structure (Credit Special Ad Category)

When running under Meta Special Ad Category — Credit:
- Must declare Special Ad Category at campaign level
- No: Age exclusions, gender exclusions, ZIP code exclusions, interest exclusions based on demographics
- Can: Use geographic (city/state/country), language, behavioral interests (not demographic proxies)
- Can: Lookalike audiences (Special Ad Category Audience = limited)
- Cannot: Exclude any protected class characteristics

```
Account (Special Ad Category: Credit)
├── Campaign: Prospecting — Lookalike (CBO)
│   ├── Ad Set: LAL 1% Approved Customers (Special Ad Audience)
│   └── Ad Set: LAL 2-3% Approved Customers
│
├── Campaign: Intent Prospecting
│   └── Ad Set: Behavioral — financial interests, loan seekers
│
└── Campaign: Retargeting
    ├── Ad Set: Application starters (7-day)
    └── Ad Set: Product page visitors (14-day)
```

## Creative Strategy

### Compliant Messaging by Product

| Product Type          | Strong Angle                                      | Required Disclaimer in Copy                    |
|-----------------------|---------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| Personal loans        | "Rates from X% APR — see your rate in 2 min"     | "APR ranges from X%–X%. Rate depends on creditworthiness." |
| Mortgage              | "Today's rate: [estimated range]"                | Rates subject to change; example rate shown    |
| Credit cards          | "[X]x rewards on every purchase"                 | Annual fee; APR must appear                    |
| Investment platform   | "Grow your portfolio — start with $[X]"          | "All investing involves risk."                 |
| Business banking      | "Zero fees. Real support."                       | "Subject to terms and conditions"              |
| Accounting software   | "Close your books 5x faster"                     | "Results vary"                                 |
| Insurance             | "Coverage from $[X]/month"                       | "Rate depends on coverage selected and eligibility" |

### Ad Format Priorities

| Format                  | Use Case                                                 | Notes                                         |
|-------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Google RSA              | Product intent capture; compliance-reviewed headlines   | Embed required disclosure in description      |
| Meta Lead Gen Form      | Pre-qualified leads; pre-fill from profile data         | Include consent language; comply with TCPA    |
| Meta Single Image       | Awareness + retargeting                                 | Clean product-focused creative; rates/badge   |
| YouTube (15–30 sec)     | Brand trust for high-consideration products            | Disclaimers must be on-screen                 |
| LinkedIn Sponsored      | B2B fintech; SMB banking                               | CFO/Finance Director targeting                |

### Trust-Building Creative Elements

- **Regulatory logos**: FDIC insured, NCUA, state licensing badges
- **Security signals**: "256-bit encryption", "Bank-level security"
- **Third-party validation**: App Store rating, award badges, Forbes/TechCrunch mentions
- **Speed proof**: "Apply in 5 minutes", "Approval in 24 hours"
- **Comparison tables**: Side-by-side vs. traditional bank/competitor (factual only)

## Targeting Guidelines

### Google Search Keyword Priorities

**High-intent buying keywords:**
- `best [product type] rates [year]`
- `apply for [product] online`
- `[product] compare rates`
- `[product] for [credit score range / situation]`
- `[competitor] vs [product category]`

**Research/calculator keywords:**
- `[product] calculator`
- `how much [product] can I afford`
- `[product] eligibility check`
- `is [financial action] worth it`

**Competitor keywords:**
- `[big bank] alternatives`
- `better than [legacy institution]`
- `[competitor] APR`

**Negative Keywords:**
- Job-seeking terms
- `free` (if not free product)
- Academic/research terms (`thesis`, `research paper`, `statistics`)
- Unqualified demographic modifiers (`bad credit`, `no credit` if targeting good-credit products)

### Meta Targeting (Standard — Non-Credit)

**For non-credit financial products (fintech tools, accounting, B2B):**
- Behavioral: "Small business owners", "Early-stage investors", "Business decision makers"
- Interest: Personal finance, investing, entrepreneurship, accounting
- Demographics: Income brackets (US market; available through select partners)
- Lookalike: Best source = completed applications / funded accounts

### LinkedIn Targeting for B2B Finance

- Company size: 10–500 employees (SMB banking / accounting); 500+ (enterprise fintech)
- Job function: Finance, Accounting, Operations, C-suite
- Seniority: Manager, Director, VP, CXO (financial decision-makers)
- Industry: All industries + specialty by product (e.g., Accounting software → all industries; Business banking → startup-heavy industries)

## KPI Targets

### Primary KPIs

| Metric                     | Target Range (Industry Benchmark)           | Notes                                         |
|----------------------------|---------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Application CPL (consumer) | $30 – $100 (fintech); $50–$150 (lending)   | Varies by product risk profile                |
| Approved Account CPA       | $80 – $250                                  | Application-to-approval rate = 20–60%         |
| Cost per Funded Account    | $150 – $500 (lending)                      | Funded rate varies 30–70% of approved         |
| CAC (B2B fintech/SaaS)     | $100 – $400 (SMB); $500 – $2,000 (mid-market)| ACV-dependent                               |
| LTV:CAC Ratio              | ≥ 3:1                                       | Finance products have high LTV if retained    |

### Secondary KPIs

| Metric                     | Target                                      | Notes                                         |
|----------------------------|---------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Application Start Rate     | 5% – 20% (landing page)                    | Pre-qualification reduces abandonment         |
| Application Completion Rate| 40% – 70%                                  | Form length inversely impacts completion      |
| Fraud Rate                 | Monitor; alert if > 5% of applications     | High fraud signals audience quality issue     |
| CTR (Google Search)        | 2% – 6%                                    | Finance is competitive; 3%+ is respectable   |
| LTV-Adjusted ROAS          | 4x – 8x (on LTV basis)                    | First-year revenue ROAS understates true value|
