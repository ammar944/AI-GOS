# Real Estate Media Plan Template

> Source: Industry benchmark aggregates (2024–2025). Applies to residential real estate agents,
> brokerages, mortgage lenders, property management companies, and commercial real estate firms.
> COMPLIANCE NOTE: Real estate is a Special Ad Category on Meta; Fair Housing Act applies.

---

## Compliance Requirements (Read First)

### Fair Housing Act (FHA) Compliance

The Fair Housing Act prohibits discrimination in housing advertising based on:
Race, Color, National Origin, Religion, Sex, Familial Status, Disability

**In practice for digital advertising:**
- Cannot exclude audiences based on the above protected characteristics
- Meta: Must declare "Housing" Special Ad Category — eliminates age, gender, ZIP exclusions
- Google: Fair housing targeting restrictions apply to housing ads
- Cannot use language implying preference for any protected class
- Must include Equal Housing Opportunity statement in ads and landing pages

### Required Disclosures

- Equal Housing Opportunity logo (EHO) in display ads where space permits
- Licensed agent/broker disclosure (by state; e.g., "Licensed Real Estate Broker [State]")
- Mortgage ads: APR, terms, and eligibility disclosures (see Finance template)
- "Artist rendering" for pre-construction property images

```
├── Campaign: Buyer Intent

│
├── Campaign: Seller Intent
│   ├── Ad Group: Sell my home [city]
│   ├── Ad Group: Home valuation / what is my home worth
│   └── Ad Group: List my home [city]
│
├── Campaign: Rental / Property Management
│   ├── Ad Group: Apartments for rent [city]

│
└── Campaign: Remarketing Display
    ├── Ad Group: Property listing viewers (7-day)
    └── Ad Group: CMA/valuation page visitors (30-day)
```

### Meta Ads Structure (Special Ad Category: Housing)

When running under Special Ad Category — Housing, the following apply:
- Age: Cannot exclude any age group (13+ minimum applies automatically)
- Gender: Cannot exclude; all genders reached
- ZIP code / postal: Cannot use ZIP-level exclusions
- Radius: Minimum radius varies (often 15+ miles in US)
- Lookalike: Special Ad Audience (limited; based on seed without demographic components)

```
Account (Special Ad Category: Housing)
├── Campaign: Listing Showcase (CBO)
│   ├── Ad Set: Geo area — broad (city + metro)
│   └── Format: Carousel listing photos or video tour
│
├── Campaign: Lead Generation (CBO)
│   ├── Ad Set: Home buyer intent (behavioral)
│   └── Format: Lead gen form (download buyers guide / CMA request)
│
├── Campaign: Seller Lead Gen (CBO)
│   └── Ad Set: Homeowner intent; geo-targeted
│       Format: "What is your home worth?" CTA
│
└── Campaign: Retargeting (Safe audiences)
    └── Ad Set: Website visitors — general (30-day)
```

## Creative Strategy

### Property Listing Ads

| Format              | Best Use                                              | Notes                                         |
|---------------------|-------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Photo carousel      | Listing showcase (4–6 best photos)                   | First photo = hero exterior; include price badge |
| Video walkthrough   | Luxury and unique properties; virtual tour           | 60–120 sec; drone footage for exterior        |
| Single image        | Lead gen offer (CMA, buyer guide)                    | Bold text overlay with offer                  |
| Dynamic listing ads | Large inventory (property management, brokerage)     | Auto-generated from MLS/property feed         |

### Messaging Angles by Audience

| Audience               | Message Angle                                           | CTA                                         |
|------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Active buyers          | "3 bed, 2 bath — [price] — See homes available now"   | View Listings / Schedule Tour               |
| First-time buyers      | "Ready to buy? We'll guide you every step of the way" | Free Buyer Consultation                     |
| Sellers (equity-rich)  | "Your home may be worth more than you think"           | Free Home Valuation / CMA                   |
| Expired listing sellers| "Why your home didn't sell — and what to do next"     | Free Listing Strategy Call                  |
| Renters → buyers       | "Your rent is paying someone else's mortgage"          | Pre-Qualification / Buyer Guide             |
| Investors              | "Cap rate X%; turnkey investment in [market]"         | Investment Portfolio Consultation            |

### Visual Guidelines

- High-quality photography is non-negotiable — professional photos required
- Drone/aerial shots significantly improve luxury and land property engagement
- Include neighborhood/lifestyle photos alongside property shots
- Video walkthroughs: 60–120 seconds; include interior + exterior + neighborhood
- Price overlays: Include asking price in listing carousel ads when not prohibited by MLS

## Targeting Guidelines

### Google Search Keywords

**Buyer intent:**
- `homes for sale [city/neighborhood]`
- `[city] real estate`
- `houses for sale [zip code]`
- `[property type] for sale [area]`
- `real estate agent [city]`
- `best realtor [city]`

**Seller intent:**
- `sell my house [city]`
- `home value [city]` / `what is my home worth`
- `real estate listing [city]`
- `how to sell my house`
- `CMA [city]` / `comparable sales [city]`

**Renter intent:**
- `apartments for rent [city]`
- `[property type] rentals [area]`
- `homes for rent [city]`

**Negative Keywords:**
- `foreclosure` (unless you specialize in this)
- `for sale by owner`, `FSBO` (unless targeting them)
- `jobs`, `real estate career`, `real estate license`
- Unserved geographic areas

### Meta Targeting (Special Ad Category Housing)

Available targeting (post-Special Ad Category declaration):
- Geographic: City/metro area (minimum radius applies)
- Behavioral: "Likely to move" (Facebook behavioral), "Online real estate searchers"
- Interests: Home buying, real estate investment, home improvement (broad)
- Special Ad Audience: Lookalike equivalent built without demographic signals

### Local Organic SEO Parallel Strategy

Note: For real estate, Google Business Profile optimization + Zillow/Realtor.com profile are critical complements to paid media. Paid media performance improves when organic presence is strong (brand searches, review signals).

## KPI Targets

### Primary KPIs

| Metric                     | Target Range (Industry Benchmark)          | Notes                                         |
|----------------------------|--------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Lead CPL (buyer inquiry)   | $20 – $80 (Google); $15 – $50 (Meta)      | Lead = form fill or call; quality varies widely|
| Cost per Seller Lead       | $30 – $120                                | Seller leads higher value; lower volume       |
| Lead-to-Appointment Rate   | 15% – 35%                                 | Speed of follow-up is primary driver          |
| Appointment-to-Client Rate | 30% – 60%                                 | Agent skill factor                            |
| Cost per Closed Transaction| $500 – $3,000                             | Blended; luxury deals justify higher CPT      |

| Metric                     | Target                                     | Notes                                         |
|----------------------------|--------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Listing Carousel CTR       | 1.5% – 4% (Meta)                          | Visual quality = primary driver               |
| Form Completion Rate       | 20% – 40%                                 | Shorter forms (name + phone + timeline)       |
