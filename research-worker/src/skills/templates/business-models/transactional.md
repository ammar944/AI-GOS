# Transactional Business-Model Template

> Applies to local service businesses, one-time-purchase services, professional services with booking-based revenue. Examples: plumbers, HVAC, legal services, home cleaning, personal trainers, one-off consultation businesses.

**Signals this model applies:** service delivered offline or single-session, booking-driven revenue, local or regional TAM, one-time or ad-hoc purchase pattern (no subscription).

---

## Funnel

Impression → Click → Lead / Call → Booking → Service Delivered → Repeat

**Stage definitions:**
- Lead / Call: form fill or phone call
- Booking: confirmed appointment on calendar
- Service Delivered: job completed, payment received
- Repeat: follow-on service or referral

---

## KPI Framework (directional — NO client-specific targets)

| KPI | What it measures | Drivers | Industry range (benchmark) |
|---|---|---|---|
| CPL (cost per lead) | Paid efficiency | Creative + locality + timing | $15–$100 for local service |
| Call-to-book rate | Intake quality + timing | Answering speed, consultative skill | 40–70% |
| Show rate | Booking confirmation quality | Reminder systems, friction | 70–90% |
| Cost per completed job | Full-funnel efficiency | CPL × 1/(show rate × close rate) | Category-specific |
| Average job value | Pricing + upsell | Service tier, add-ons | Category-specific |
| Repeat booking rate | Service quality + retention | Service delivery + follow-up | 10–40% |

## Default Channel Mix

| Channel | Share | Role | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Google LSA (Local Services Ads) | 30–50% | Primary for service bookings | Pay-per-lead, Google-verified |
| Google Search | 20–35% | High-intent "near me" capture | Category + locality kw |
| Meta Ads | 15–25% | Lookalikes + brand awareness | Seed off past customers |
| Nextdoor / local platforms | 5–15% | Community trust signal | Hyper-local channels |

**NOT IN MIX by default:**
- LinkedIn (wrong buyer)
- TikTok (typically wrong audience for local service; exception: beauty/fitness in urban markets)
- Long-form YouTube (expensive for small budgets)

**Budget tier rules:**
- ≤£500/mo: LSA only (pay-per-lead model keeps this viable)
- £500–£2k/mo: LSA + Google search
- £2k–£5k/mo: Add Meta for lookalikes + brand awareness
- £5k+/mo: Full mix + local platforms

---

## Campaign Types

**Allowed:**
- Local Services Ads (LSA) — pay-per-lead, Google-verified business
- Google Search with call extensions + location extensions
- Google Call-only ads (mobile-first)
- Meta lead-gen forms (with strong qualifier questions)
- Nextdoor sponsored posts

**Forbidden / avoid:**
- E-commerce / catalog campaigns (no online store)
- Signup campaigns (no account creation)
- Demo-booking (no separate demo stage)

---

## Creative Approach

- **Trust signals** — "licensed + insured", years in business, family-owned, Google rating (4.8★ from 200+ reviews)
- **Speed** — "same-day service", "24h response", "arriving in 30 minutes"
- **Locality** — city / neighborhood name in every headline
- **Before/after** — plumbing repair, lawn care, cleaning (if visually demonstrable)
- **Call-first** — phone number visible, click-to-call CTAs

---

## Gotchas

- Lead-to-completed-job rate depends on intake team + scheduling — not the media plan
- Don't forecast revenue in the plan — too many variables outside paid media
- Locality hygiene essential — wrong geo targeting burns budget fast
- Seasonality is high — HVAC peaks summer/winter, landscaping spring/fall, etc. Address in phases.
- Phone call attribution is essential — call tracking (CallRail, Invoca) required
- LSA doesn't scale linearly — budget increases often hit lead-volume ceiling before budget cap

---

## Sales cycle

Transactional cycles are short:
- Emergency services (plumbing emergency, locksmith): minutes to hours
- Scheduled services (cleaning, tune-up): same-day to 48 hours
- Considered services (legal consult, accountant): 1–7 days

Attribution windows: 7-day click / 1-day view for scheduled; same-session for emergency.
