# SLG (Sales-Led Growth) Business-Model Template

> Source: David Skok SaaS metrics + Mark Roberge Sales Acceleration Formula. Applies to SaaS, B2B services, and enterprise software with demo-required / committee-buying / sales-team-close dynamics.

**Signals this model applies:** demo required before purchase, dedicated sales team, mid-to-high ACV ($5k–$500k/yr), committee-led buying decisions, 30+ day sales cycles.

---

## Funnel

Impression → Click → Lead → MQL → SQL → Opportunity → Customer

**Stage definitions:**
- Lead: form fill or content download (not yet qualified)
- MQL: lead meets marketing qualification (ICP fit, intent signals)
- SQL: lead accepted by sales (engaged, real buyer, real timing)
- Opportunity: sales-qualified pipeline with dollar amount
- Customer: closed-won

---

## KPI Framework (directional — NO client-specific targets)

| KPI | What it measures | Drivers | Industry range (benchmark) |
|---|---|---|---|
| CPL (cost per lead) | Top-funnel paid efficiency | Creative + targeting + offer | $30–$300 for B2B SaaS |
| MQL rate | Marketing qualification quality | Targeting precision | 20–50% |
| SQL rate | Sales acceptance of leads | Lead quality + handoff process | 20–40% of MQLs |
| Opportunity rate | Sales engagement effectiveness | Sales process + discovery | 40–60% of SQLs |
| Win rate | Close capability | Offer, sales skill, pricing | 15–30% of opportunities |
| Sales cycle length | Speed of decision | Buyer complexity, offer clarity | 30–180 days |

**Never publish client-specific CAC targets.** SLG CAC depends on: sales process quality, close rate, offer strength, pricing — all outside the media plan's control. Publish benchmark ranges only.

## Default Channel Mix

| Channel | Share | Role | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn Ads | 30–45% | Primary for committee-buyer B2B | Required for mid-market+ SaaS |
| Google Search | 25–40% | Bottom-funnel high-intent | Category + competitor + branded kw |
| Meta Ads | 10–20% | Retargeting + lookalikes | Seed off CRM + closed-won |
| YouTube / Display | 5–15% | Remarketing + authority content | ABM-targeted content distribution |

**NOT IN MIX by default:**
- TikTok (rare B2B buyer)
- Snapchat / Pinterest
- Broad programmatic display

**Budget tier rules:**
- ≤£4k/mo: LinkedIn not viable (minimum ~£4k for meaningful reach). Use Google + Meta only.
- £4k–£10k: Full mix with LinkedIn at 30–35%
- £10k–£25k: LinkedIn at 35–45%, ABM targeting active
- £25k+: Add ABM vendors (6sense, Demandbase) + intent-data layering

---

## Campaign Types

**Allowed:**
- Lead-gen forms (LinkedIn + Meta)
- Search campaigns: brand, competitor, category, problem-aware
- Content syndication (gated whitepapers)
- Demo-booking campaigns (direct)
- Retargeting (site visitors, form abandons, pricing page)
- Account-based (ABM) for enterprise tier

**Forbidden / avoid:**
- Signup-based campaigns (no self-serve signup in SLG)
- E-commerce / product ads (no product catalog)
- PLG-style "try free" campaigns (contradicts demo gate)

---

## Creative Approach

- **Authority-driven** — analyst quotes (Gartner, Forrester), customer case studies with logos + metrics
- **ROI demonstration** — calculator, savings chart, payback comparison
- **Thought leadership** — gated whitepaper, webinar, research report
- **Buyer-specific messaging** — job-title-specific headlines (VP Eng sees different copy than CFO)

---

## Gotchas

- Don't publish CAC targets — depends on sales close rate, not paid media
- Don't promise lead-to-customer rates — that's the sales team's KPI
- Long sales cycles (30–180 days) require 3–6 month measurement windows
- LinkedIn CPL looks high ($100+) but acceptable for high-ACV deals — don't benchmark against Meta CPL
- Committee buyers need multi-touch — single-impression ROAS is misleading

---

## Sales cycle bounding

Sales cycle must respect the buying committee structure:
- Single decision-maker: 14–45 days
- 3–5 stakeholder committee: 30–90 days
- Enterprise with procurement: 60–180 days
- Regulated industries: 120–365 days

Do not generate a shorter cycle than the committee structure requires (unlike PLG where the trial CAPS the cycle, SLG has a FLOOR from the committee).
