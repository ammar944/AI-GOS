# Info Products / Online Courses Media Plan Template

> Source: Industry benchmark aggregates (2024–2025). Applies to online courses, coaching
> programs, membership sites, webinars, ebooks, and digital products. Includes both direct
> sales and webinar funnel models.

---

## Platform Mix

| Platform           | Budget Share | Role                                              | Priority |
|--------------------|--------------|---------------------------------------------------|----------|
| Meta Ads (FB/IG)   | 35% – 50%    | Cold acquisition; webinar registrations; VSL traffic | Primary |
| Google Search      | 20% – 30%    | Bottom-funnel; topic + course intent keywords    | Primary  |
| YouTube Ads        | 15% – 25%    | VSL / long-form video; audience education        | Secondary|
| TikTok Ads         | 10% – 15%    | Content amplification; Gen Z/Millennial audience  | Secondary|
| Google Display     | 5% – 10%     | Retargeting; content site contextual targeting   | Support  |

**Model-Based Adjustments:**
- Webinar funnel: Meta dominant (50%+); YouTube for pre-webinar video content
- Direct-to-course (no webinar): Google Search + Meta balanced
- High-ticket coaching ($2K+): Meta + YouTube for nurture; direct response focus
- Low-ticket ebook/course (<$97): Meta + TikTok for volume; focus on creative testing
- Membership site: Meta retargeting heavy; email + paid hybrid

## Funnel Architecture

### Webinar Funnel (Most Common for $200–$2,000 Products)

```
Cold Traffic
└── Meta/YouTube Ad (problem-aware hook)
    └── Webinar Registration Page
        └── Webinar (live or automated/evergreen)
            ├── Offer Presentation (sales pitch at end)
            └── Replay + Cart Open (3–7 day window)
                ├── Email nurture sequence
                └── Retargeting ads (non-buyers who registered)
                    └── Purchase / Enrollment
                        └── Upsell sequence
```

### Direct Response Funnel (Low-ticket and Mid-ticket)

```
Cold Traffic
└── Ad (benefit-focused hook)
    └── Sales Page / VSL (Video Sales Letter)
        ├── Purchase
        └── Non-buyer → Retargeting
            └── Abandoned cart ad
                └── Purchase or Email nurture
```

## Creative Strategy

### Ad Types by Funnel Stage

| Stage            | Format                          | Duration / Specs | Message                                         |
|------------------|---------------------------------|------------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| Cold awareness   | Video (hook + pain agitate)    | 15–60 sec        | "If you struggle with [problem]..."             |
| Webinar reg.     | Video + image                   | 15–30 sec video  | Webinar benefit + "Register free"               |
| VSL traffic      | Video (long-form tease)         | 30–90 sec        | Preview of transformation; "Watch the free training" |
| Retargeting      | Single image / short video      | 15–30 sec        | Urgency; testimonial; objection handling        |
| Cart open        | Single image + countdown        | Static or GIF    | "Enrollment closes [date]"                      |
| Past buyer upsell| Personalized image/video        | Short (15 sec)   | "You've done X. The next step is..."            |

### Info Product Creative Formulas

**Hook formulas (first 3 seconds) for cold video:**
1. Counterintuitive statement: "Most people who want to [goal] are doing it completely backwards."
2. Results declaration: "Here's how I went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe]."
3. Mistake exposure: "Stop doing [common mistake] if you want [goal]."
4. Relatable identity: "If you're a [person type] who wants [goal] but struggles with [obstacle]..."
5. Social proof hook: "[X] people have already [achieved result] using this method."

**Video length guidelines:**
- TikTok/Reels cold: 15–30 sec (pure hook and CTA)
- Facebook In-Feed: 60–90 sec (problem + authority + soft CTA)
- YouTube pre-roll: 30–60 sec (skip-proof hook in first 5 sec; full message for engaged viewers)
- YouTube VSL teaser: 90 sec – 3 min (deep engagement filter; only interested watch through)

### Google Search Keyword Strategy

**Bottom-funnel course intent:**
- `learn [skill] online`
- `best [topic] course`
- `[skill] online course [year]`
- `[topic] certification program`
- `[mentor/influencer name] course`

**Problem-aware keywords:**
- `how to [achieve goal]`
- `how to [solve problem]`
- `[problem] solution / tips`

**Webinar-specific:**
- `free [topic] webinar`
- `[topic] training online`
- `[topic] masterclass`

### YouTube Custom Intent Audiences

Build custom intent audiences for YouTube targeting based on:
- URLs of competitor course websites
- Keywords people searched before watching related content
- YouTube channels in your niche (viewers of those channels)

## Budget Guidelines

### Minimum Monthly Budget by Product Price Point

| Product Price Point | Minimum Monthly Budget | Notes                                           |
|---------------------|------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| < $97 (low ticket)  | $2,000 – $5,000        | High volume needed; creative testing intensive  |
| $97 – $500 (mid)    | $3,000 – $8,000        | Webinar funnel common; email nurture critical   |
| $500 – $2,000 (mid-high) | $5,000 – $15,000 | Test multiple angles; video investment worthwhile |
| $2,000+ (high-ticket) | $8,000 – $30,000      | Lower volume; higher CPL acceptable; quality leads |

### Webinar Funnel Economics

Work backwards from registration goal:
1. Determine target registrations per webinar run
2. Estimate CPL (webinar registration): $5 – $25 (varies by niche and platform)
3. Calculate budget needed: Target registrations × CPL
4. Estimate show rate: 25% – 40% of registrants
5. Estimate webinar close rate: 5% – 20% of attendees
6. Project revenue: Attendees × close rate × price

**Example calculation:**
- Goal: $50,000 webinar revenue
- Price: $997
- Registrants needed: Work backwards → 50 sales ÷ 5% close × 30% show = 3,333 registrants
- At $12 CPL: 3,333 × $12 = ~$40,000 budget → if ROAS > 1.25 = viable

## KPI Targets

### Webinar Funnel KPIs

| Metric                     | Target Range (Industry Benchmark)          | Notes                                         |
|----------------------------|--------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Webinar Registration CPL   | $5 – $25                                   | Lower for broad niches; higher for specialized |
| Show Rate (live webinars)  | 25% – 45%                                 | Higher for smaller, niche audiences           |
| Webinar Close Rate         | 5% – 20%                                   | Depends on quality of webinar and offer       |
| ROAS (webinar spend)       | 2x – 5x                                    | Breakeven minimum 1.2x; scale at 2x+          |
| Retargeting ROAS           | 4x – 10x                                   | Retargeting warm registrants highest ROAS     |

### Direct Response Funnel KPIs

| Metric                     | Target Range (Industry Benchmark)          | Notes                                         |
|----------------------------|--------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Cost per Sale (low-ticket) | 20% – 40% of product price                | At $97 product: $20–$40 CPS                  |
| ROAS (direct)              | 2x – 4x (cold); 5x – 10x (retargeting)   | Video creative drives ROAS variance           |
| Landing Page Conv Rate     | 1% – 5% (sales page)                      | Long-form VSL pages: 2–8%; short pages: 1–3% |
| Cart Abandonment Recovery  | 10% – 30% via retargeting                 | Multi-touch email + ad retargeting            |

### Secondary KPIs

| Metric                     | Target                                     | Notes                                         |
|----------------------------|--------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Video Hook Rate (3-sec view)| 30% – 60% of impressions                 | Below 30% = hook needs rewrite               |
| Video Hold Rate (to 25%)   | 40% – 70%                                 | Indicates ad holds attention                  |
| Email Open Rate (post-reg) | 25% – 45%                                 | First email (confirmation) should be 50%+    |
| Upsell Take Rate           | 10% – 30%                                 | Order bumps on checkout page                 |
| Refund Rate                | < 5% – 10%                                | Higher refund = expectation mismatch in ads  |
