# Copy Rules

## Hard Bans

- No fabricated proof, testimonials, metrics, customer outcomes, or pricing claims.
- No forbidden claims from `locked_brief.fields.forbiddenClaims`.
- No copied competitor hooks.
- No placeholders: unknown, TBD, n/a, scaffold, TODO, or lorem ipsum.
- No em dashes in finished copy.

## Quality Checks

The deterministic quality gate must check:

- banned phrases
- template openers
- rule-of-three phrasing
- corporate filler
- chatbot closers
- platform character limits

Warnings can remain in `quality_gate.violations`, but platform limit failures and forbidden claim leaks block the pack.

## Voice

Use direct, human copy. Keep claims narrow. Prefer a true, small claim over a sweeping unsourced claim.
