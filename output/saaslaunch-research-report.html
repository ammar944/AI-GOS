Strategic Narrative

SaaSLaunch sits in a differentiated but under-communicated position: it is the only player in its competitive set offering a complete, integrated client acquisition system rather than a single-channel execution service. Hey Digital owns creative and paid, Sales Captain owns outbound sequences — neither owns the full revenue system narrative.

The paid media strategy must do two jobs simultaneously: (1) make the ICP feel understood by naming their exact frustration (fragmented vendors, unpredictable pipeline, agency disappointment), and (2) anchor every touchpoint to a specific, credible outcome ($1M ARR, SQLs, closed deals) to clear the skepticism barrier.

At $10K-$30K monthly budget, a LinkedIn-primary / Google Search-secondary / Meta retargeting structure is the right architecture. The single highest-leverage action before any media spend increases is fixing the demo landing page.
